Itinerary

Suggested arrival in Davao: Before 8:30 am
Suggested departure from Davao: 8 pm onwards

The rates are until Dec. 15 only. Holiday rates will be applied from Dec. 16 to January 3.

Day 1 and day 3 can be interchange depending on your flight details

DAY 1 - AIRPORT PICK-UP AND DAVAO COUNTRYSIDE TOUR (8 HOURS)

  • Eden Nature Park
  • Philippine Eagle center
  • Malagos Garden Resort
    • Malagos Chocolate Museum
  • Check-in hotel after tour

DAY 2 - PEARL FARM BEACH RESORT OVERNIGHT

  • Pearl Farm Beach Resort
  • Malipano Island

DAY 3 - DAVAO CITY TOUR (6 HOURS)

  • Lon Wa Buddhist Temple (not available on Sunday)
  • Upside Down House Museum
  • Chinatown And Magsaysay Fruit Market
  • San Pedro Cathedral
  • Bone Collectors Museum (not available on Sunday)
  • Museo Dabawenyo (not available on Sunday)
  • Running tour of Central 911
  • Duterte's House
  • Jack's Ridge
  • Shrine Of The Holy Infant Jesus Of Prague
  • Davao Crocodile Park
  • Butterfly house
  • Tribu K'Mindanawan fire show (5:30pm - weekends only)
  • Airport transfers
