Itinerary

  • Talikud Island:
    • Angel's Cove (swimming/ snorkeling)
    • Babu Santa (swimming)
    • Coral Garden
    • Isla Reta Beach Resort
  • Samal Island:
    • Pass by Malipano Island
    • Pass by Pearl Farm Beach resort
    • Taclobo Giant Clam Sanctuary
    • Wishing Island

Inclusions

  • Outrigger banca for island hopping
  • Services of a tourist guide
  • Environmental fees, permits, and other related fees
  • Packed lunch
  • All applicable entrance fees as specified in the itinerary

Conditions

  • Guests will shoulder other expenses not specified in the tour inclusions
  • Guests will shoulder the aquasports rental fee at the resorts

Extra notes

  • It is advisable to wear light and comfortable clothes and aqua shoes during the trip
  • Fixed rate applies for 20 persons and above
  • Snorkelling gears are for rent (Subject to availability)
  • Ideal to book this tour 7 days advance for banca availability
