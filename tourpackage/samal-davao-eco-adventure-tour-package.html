Itinerary

Suggested arrival in Davao: Before 12 noon
Suggested departure from Davao: 7 pm onwards

DAY 1 - AIRPORT PICK-UP AND CITY TOUR (6 HOURS)

  • Lon Wa Buddhist Temple
  • Upside Down House Museum
  • Chinatown And Magsaysay Fruit Market
  • San Pedro Cathedral
  • Bone Collectors Museum (not available on Sunday)
  • Museo Dabawenyo (weekdays only)
  • Running tour of Central 911
  • Duterte's House
  • Jack's Ridge
  • Shrine Of The Holy Infant Jesus Of Prague
  • Davao Crocodile Park
  • Tribu K'Mindanawan fire show (weekends only)
  • Check-in hotel after tour

DAY 2 - SAMAL ISLAND OUTDOOR AND ECOTOUR

Samal Inland:  

  • Paradise Beach Resort
  • Uraya Beach resort (swimming and lunch)
  • Monfort Bat Sanctuary (sightseeing)
  • Hagimit Falls (swimming)

Samal Island Hopping:

  • Vanishing Sand Bar (swimming)
  • Pass by Pearl Farm Beach Resort
  • Pass by Malipano Island
  • Taclobo Giant Clam Sanctuary (snorkeling)
  • Narnia Wishing Island (sightseeing/swimming)

DAY 3 - DAVAO COUNTRYSIDE TOUR (8 HOURS)

  • Hotel Checkout
  • Eden Nature Park
  • Philippine Eagle center
  • Malagos Garden Resort
    • Malagos Chocolate Museum
  • Airport Transfers
